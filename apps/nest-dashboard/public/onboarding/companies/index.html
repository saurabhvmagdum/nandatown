Corporate contributor

Add your module to NANDA Town.

You run a platform, and you want to be part of the emerging network of AI agents. Show how your product works inside NANDA Town. No requirement to open-source. An open hook, an SDK, or API access is enough.

The invitation

Add your module to nandatown.projectnanda.org. Your company does not have to contribute its own open-source code, an open hook is enough, whether that is SDK or API access. Keep your platform private and let agents connect to it.

1 Pick how you show up

Choose your contribution

You can do one of these, or several. We would love real integration, but a demo or a written case is a strong start and we can grow from there.

An open hook or module preferred

4

A written case

A detailed write-up of how your platform improves NANDA Town, and why an internet of NANDA agents is a strong ecosystem for your business. Submit a write-up →

2 Why plug into an agent network

Your service, reachable by every agent

NANDA Town is a shared registry, not a single project. Once your module is listed, any agent in the network can find and use it, and other teams can build on top of it.

Distribution: agents across the network discover your service automatically.
Composability: other contributors can build products that call your hook, the way a demo team can build on a payment or routing provider.
Control: you expose only the interface you choose. Your platform and IP stay yours.
